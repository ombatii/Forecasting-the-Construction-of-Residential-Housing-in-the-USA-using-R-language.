Forecasting the Construction of Residential Housing in the USA.

Business Understading

We intend to learn to estimate future trends in the American housing market for small and medium-sized dwellings based on previous patterns and present market circumstances. We also seek to improve our knowledge of the variables influencing small and medium-sized housing demand and supply, such as interest rates, economic growth, demography, and government regulations. This forecasting will also aid in the identification of potential risks and uncertainties in the housing market, such as price changes, housing oversupply or undersupply, and altering consumer preferences.
